SSVIT MUN Workshop: What You Missed

What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a fun and educational experience where students take on the roles of diplomats from different countries. As a delegate, you'll debate real-world issues, negotiate with others, and work together to find solutions, just like in the actual United Nations!

It's an exciting way to develop your skills in public speaking, teamwork, problem-solving, and diplomacy while learning about global challenges. Whether you're discussing climate change, international security, or human rights, MUN brings together passionate young people from around the world to collaborate and create change.

What do delegates do in MUN?

Delegates research their assigned country or issue, write speeches, and actively participate in debates. They work with other delegates to draft resolutions, propose solutions, and ultimately pass agreements that can make a real difference. It's an incredible opportunity to challenge yourself, make new friends, and be part of something truly global.
